CADRE_launch.py¶

class CADRE.CADRE_launch.CADRE_Launch(n=200)[source]¶

Bases: openmdao.main.assembly.Assembly

Allows for analysis of the launch parameters of CADRE.

Considers multiple launch parameters and their effects on coverage of the Earth’s thermosphere.

Ultimately, a launch that provides the most uniform sampling is favorable, which is expected to be given by a polar orbit (Inclination near 90). Other launch parameters are probably not very useful in comparison.
